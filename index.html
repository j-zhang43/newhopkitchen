About Us

New Hop Shing Kitchen is a Chinese Restaurant that has been open dated back to the
19th century in the town of Passaic.

Focusing on customers' desires of unique flavors and cuisines, the restaurant is a family
business making each dish in our own kitchen from scratch to fulfill each and every
customer's taste.

We honor our customers by providing them with the freshest, most delicious, and
authentic meal experience possible.
